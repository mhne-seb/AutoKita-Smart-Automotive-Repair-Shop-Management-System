# AutoKita: A Smart Automotive Repair Shop Management System

AutoKita is a capstone project focused on developing a smart automotive repair shop management system designed to streamline business operations through efficient management of services, customer communication, and digital record keeping. The system replaces traditional paper-based processes with a centralized platform to improve operational efficiency and service quality.
